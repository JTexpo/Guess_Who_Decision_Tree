    How to use this app
    When you load the page, you'll see a question with "Yes" and "No" buttons. The question relates to a character from Castle Crashers.

    If you're not familiar with the characters, a portrait of all of them is displayed below. Click on any portrait to view detailed information about that character, which might help answer the question.

    To play, simply respond to the "Yes" or "No" questions, and watch as the AI tries to guess the character you're thinking of!

    When you're finished, you can click the "New Game" button to start a new game.

    What is a decision tree?
    Decision trees are similar to "if-else" statements; however, in this example, the decision tree is created programmatically.

    This approach ensures the decision tree achieves the smallest possible depth by splitting based on the feature with the largest variance.

    Decision trees excel in well-defined situations where the goal is either to approximate the classification of a new object or to directly classify an existing one.
